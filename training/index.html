Training: overview

Welcome to the RCVS's bespoke content style training!

Completing this training will give you the skills and know-how you need in order to consistently create content in the RCVS style. It will give you familiarity with our content style and our content style guide. It will make sure that you know where to look for answers and how to ask for help if you can't find what you're looking for.

To test your knowledge and understanding, complete these three units of training: watch the short videos and complete a quiz for each. Submit each one when you're done and we'll get a record that you've completed them.
