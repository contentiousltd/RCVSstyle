RCVS content style guide

This guide is for everyone creating, or contributing to, RCVS content. It explains how we communicate accurately and clearly. It also demonstrates how we convey our personality consistently and authentically. And it helps us to connect with our audience in positive, meaningful and efficient ways.

Use the navigation to get to exactly what you need quickly and easily, or, if you want a quick overview, start with our Essentials section.

Our style guide is a living resource. Organisational priorities and the world around us will evolve, and our content will need to evolve too. If anything can be improved, please let us know.

If you’re looking for an answer and can’t find it here, get in touch with the Comms team. We’ll be happy to help.
